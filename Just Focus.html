The Experience

This project was developed in 8 hours during a challenge with a friend, based on the theme “mindfulness.” The core mechanic allows the player to view the entire map for three seconds before it fades into darkness. The objective is to reach the exit using memory and focus, with each level progressively increasing in difficulty.
